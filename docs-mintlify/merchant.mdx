In the Merchant tab, you can edit your business information, such as your logo and name. You can also define a account currency, BoomFi fees, and a Webhook URL.

![](/images/docs/0480ce91a1f602ad9cbce0fd6b8d591ee35a1d6ef054b880b7a353f6cf2e6383-merchant.png)

### Webhook URL

Setting a Webhook URL enables BoomFi to communicate asynchronously with your integration, ensuring you are promptly notified whenever significant events occur. This mechanism will enhance the efficiency of information dissemination and streamline the process of staying updated on various events.
