## Using BoomFi's on-ramp

To purchase crypto with BoomFi's on-ramp solution, users will undergo the following steps:

1. Access the swap link: [https://swap.boomfi.xyz/](https://swap.boomfi.xyz/)
2. Select the desired amount of fiat or cryptocurrency they wish to purchase. The BoomFi on-ramp interface will provide a real-time indicative quote.
3. Log in or create an account using email Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
4. Complete a quick and straightforward Know Your Customer (KYC) process if the user is new to BoomFi.
5. Confirm the purchase amount by submitting card details.

After this, BoomFi will confirm the card payment, execute the order at the final price, and deliver the crypto to the user.

<Callout type="info">
  By accessing the link provided in the first step, you can try the solution yourself.
</Callout>
